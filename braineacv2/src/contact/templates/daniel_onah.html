Biography

Dr Onah, a native born of Nigeria is an academic of strong international standing in Computer Science and Computer Systems Engineering (Software Systems). Dr Onah received BSc (Honors) degree in Computer Science from Nigeria and received the MSc (Eng.) degree in Computer Systems Engineering with specialization in Software Systems from the University of East London, United Kingdom in 2011 and the Doctor of Philosophy in Computer Science from the University of Warwick, United Kingdom in 2017.

I joined Dr Mina Ryten's Lab at UCL Institute of Neurology in August 2017. I will focus on understanding the transcription factors of gene expression in post-morten human tissues of brain. By gaining deeper understanding and insight into this research we hope we are able to create useful software package which can promote more stable and accurate regulation of gene expression. This will benefit the application in bioinformatics especially in molecular neuroscience.
