Research Interests

  • Machine Learning in human genetics and genomics.
  • Gene expression based network models for disease.
  • Statistical assessment of complex models of gene interactions.

    • Biography

    Juan A. Botía is machine learning researcher interested in computational methods for genetics and genomics data analysis in the context of neurological diseases. He leads co-expression network analysis within the United Kingdom Brain Expression Consortium (UKBEC). His academic background is in computing science and artificial intelligence. He currently holds a position as Reader in Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence in Universidad de Murcia, Spain. He actively collaborates at Ryten Lab by coordinating the research on network models based on gene expression and genetic data. He also applies machine learning methods for the characterization of gene-phenotype association for the aim to contribute to deciphering neurodegenerative diseases’ aetiology.
